Pulp Fiction

Directed by Quinten Tarantino

Pulp Fiction written in 1994 is Quinten Tarantino's 3rd directed movie
and is considered to be one of his greatest films to date. Pulp Fiction is
comprised of a few seperate story lines that are chopped into segments and mixed
together both chronologically and by story line. It tells the story of two hitmen,
Jules Winnfield and Vincent Vega, both of whom work for a mysterious
boss known as Marsellus Wallace (who does NOT look like a b#tch).

The second story line follows professional boxer Butch Coolidge. The peak
of this story occurs when Butch is being paid by Marsellus Wallace to purposly
get beat for a large sum of money. Butch not only doesn't throw the fight,
but kills his opponent in the ring and flees the country with his girlfriend
Fabienne.
